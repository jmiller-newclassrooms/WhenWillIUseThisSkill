Math in Context: Understand Absolute Value

Use Case 1: Cryptozoology (Study of Hidden Animals)

Cryptozoologists investigate reports of mysterious creatures, including the depth at which they are believed to live. Understanding absolute value helps them analyze the potential depths of these cryptic animals.

Use Case 2: Ghost Hunting

Ghost hunters use various instruments to measure paranormal activity, including changes in temperature. Absolute value helps them interpret temperature fluctuations, whether they are increases or decreases.

Use Case 3: Ufology (Study of Unidentified Flying Objects)

Ufologists analyze sightings of UFOs, which often involve rapid changes in altitude. Understanding absolute value helps them quantify these changes and assess the capabilities of unidentified flying objects.

Use Case 4: Parapsychology (Study of Psychic Phenomena)

Parapsychologists study psychic abilities, including telekinesis, which can involve both the movement of objects up or down. Absolute value helps them measure the strength of telekinetic forces.

Use Case 5: Exobotany (Study of Extraterrestrial Plants)

Exobotanists explore the possibility of plant life on other planets. Absolute value is useful for interpreting temperature variations on alien worlds, whether they are extreme cold or extreme heat.
