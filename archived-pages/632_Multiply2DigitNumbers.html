Math in Context: Multiply 2-digit Whole Numbers

Archaeology

Archaeologists study ancient civilizations and artifacts. They use multiplication to calculate the area of excavation sites for archaeological digs, ensuring efficient planning.

Architecture

Architects design buildings and structures. They use multiplication to estimate material quantities for construction projects, ensuring accurate planning.

Astronomy

Astronomers study celestial objects and the universe. They use multiplication to calculate astronomical distances and the speed of light, contributing to space exploration.

Zoology

Zoologists study animals and their behavior. They use multiplication to estimate animal populations in wildlife habitats, aiding conservation efforts.

Game Development

Game developers create video games. They use multiplication to design game levels with grid layouts, ensuring precise placement of game elements.
